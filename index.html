Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 17/04/2025

Calee is an academic utility app developed for the students and faculty of IIT Ropar. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: We use Google Authentication to sign in users. Only institutional emails ending with @iitrpr.ac.in are accepted and those assigned as admins can login with their own email ids.
  • Name: Retrieved from the user's Google profile upon login.
  • App Data: The app fetches event schedules, CSV files, and image assets from Firebase. No other personal or sensitive data is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected email and name are used solely for authentication and personalizing the app experience. We do not store, share, or sell any personal data beyond what is mentioned above.

3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored in Firebase. Access is limited to authorized administrators only. We do not store or process any payment or sensitive personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Sign-In and Firebase for backend services. These platforms have their own privacy policies which users should also review.

5. Children's Privacy

Calee is intended for use by individuals associated with IIT Ropar. It does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the development team.
